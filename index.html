Here are a few of my favorite artists


Limp Bizkit


Limp Bizkit is an American rap rock band from Jacksonville, Florida.

The band was formed in 1994 by vocalist Fred Durst, bassist Sam Rivers, and drummer John Otto.

Guitarist Wes Borland joined the band shortly after its formation, and DJ Lethal became a member in 1996.

Linkin Park


Linkin Park is an American rock band from Agoura Hills, California.

The band was formed in 1996 by three high school friends: Mike Shinoda, Brad Delson, and Rob Bourdon.

They were later joined by Joe Hahn, Dave "Phoenix" Farrell, and Chester Bennington.

Silverchair


Silverchair were an Australian rock band, formed in 1992 in Newcastle, New South Wales, by school friends Daniel Johns, Ben Gillies and Chris Joannou.

They were originally named Innocent Criminals, but changed their name to Silverchair in 1994.

They achieved worldwide success with their debut album, Frogstomp, which was released in 1995 when the band members were only 15 years old.
